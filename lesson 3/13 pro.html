iPhone 13 and iPhone 13 mini offer many powerful features, including the most advanced dual-camera system ever on an iPhone. Flat-edged design with aerospace-grade aluminum frame, available in six stunning color finishes: Green, Satellite White, Midnight Grey, Blue, Pink, (PRODUCT)RED. Durable design includes Ceramic Shield, industry-leading IP68 water resistance and resistance to spilled liquids such as coffee, tea and sodas. A brighter Super Retina XDR display offers a maximum brightness of 800nit and a peak brightness of 1,200nit for HDR quality photos and videos. iPhone 13 mini includes a 5.4-inch Super Retina XDR display and iPhone 13 includes a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display. You can use the iPhone 13 for over two and a half hours more than the iPhone 12, and over an hour and a half more on the iPhone 13 mini compared to the iPhone 13 mini. The ultra-fast A15 Bionic chip powers a powerful new 6-core processor with 2 high-performance cores and 4 high-efficiency cores.
